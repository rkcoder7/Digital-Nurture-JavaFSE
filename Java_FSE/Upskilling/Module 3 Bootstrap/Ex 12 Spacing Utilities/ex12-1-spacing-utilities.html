Margin Utilities

m-* = all sides  |  mt-* = top  |  mb-* = bottom  |  ms-* = left  |  me-* = right  |  mx-* = horizontal  |  my-* = vertical

m-3 – margin on all sides (1rem)
mt-4 – margin-top of 1.5rem
mb-5 – margin-bottom of 3rem
mx-auto – horizontally centered (needs fixed width)
my-3 – margin top and bottom (1rem)
ms-5 – margin-start / left (3rem)

Padding Utilities

p-* = all sides  |  pt-* = top  |  pb-* = bottom  |  ps-* = left  |  pe-* = right  |  px-* = horizontal  |  py-* = vertical

p-2 – padding all sides (0.5rem)
py-5 – padding top and bottom (3rem). Notice the height!
px-4 – padding left and right (1.5rem)
pt-3 pb-1 – top 1rem, bottom 0.25rem
