Community Bulletin

Neighbourhood News

Roads & Infrastructure

The council has approved a six-month plan to resurface Maple Avenue and Oak Street. Work begins the first week of next month and is expected to cause minor delays during morning rush hour. Residents are encouraged to use alternative routes via Elm Road or the bypass until completion.

Green Spaces Initiative

Volunteers are needed for the autumn planting drive at Riverside Park. Over three hundred native trees and shrubs will be planted to support local wildlife corridors. Sessions run every Saturday morning from 8am to 12pm. All tools and refreshments are provided by the council.

Community Centre Update

The refurbished community centre will reopen next month with extended evening hours. New facilities include a recording studio for youth programmes, a coworking area for freelancers, and a commercial kitchen available for hire by local food businesses. Booking opens online from the fifteenth.

Safety & Neighbourhood Watch

The neighbourhood watch scheme has expanded to four new streets following a successful pilot. Monthly meetings are held at the fire station and all residents are welcome. The scheme has helped reduce opportunistic vehicle theft by thirty percent this year compared to the same period last year.
